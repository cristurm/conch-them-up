What is this shit?

Conch Them Up is like a shmup, but less hard-core. Made with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

But how do I play it?

  • W/A/S/D: Move
  • SPACE: Shoot
  • ESC: Pause

Survive as many enemy waves as you can to beat the highest score.

If a monster reaches the leftmost end of the screen, you loose points.

If you kill an enemy, you earn points.

The wave is completed when the progress bar is full (all green).

You die when you run out of points and the progress bar is empty (if you take damage and your score is 0, all orange).

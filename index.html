Conch Them Up!

What?

Conch Them Up is like a shmup, but less hard-core. Made with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

Instructions:

  • W/A/S/D: Move
  • SPACE: Shoot
  • ESC: Pause
Survive as many enemy waves as you can to beat the highest score. If a monster reaches your end of the screen, you loose points. If you kill an enemy, you earn points. The wave is completed when the progress bar is filled (all green). You die when the progress bar is less than empty (when you take damage when you have 0 score, all orange).
